## 🚨 Problem Landscape

Deepfakes are becoming highly realistic and scalable, making digital content unreliable in real time.  
Existing systems are reactive and fail to verify authenticity at the moment of exposure.  

➡️ This results in:
- Delayed response  
- Blind content consumption  
- Viral misinformation  
- Financial and security risks  

---

## 💡 Our Solution

We introduce a **real-time AI-powered trust inference system** that operates during content consumption.

- Passive detection via browser extension  
- On-demand verification for media  
- Trust Score + Risk Level + Confidence  
- Instant, interpretable outputs  

➡️ Moving from detection → decision intelligence
